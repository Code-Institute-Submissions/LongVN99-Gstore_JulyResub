Frequently Asked Questions

General questions

Is account registration required? Expand

Account registration at Gstore is only required if you will be selling or buying products. This ensures a valid communication channel for all parties involved in any transactions.

What is the currency used for all transactions? Expand

All prices for products in Gstore including each seller's or buyer's account balance are in USD

Is there any offer? Expand

All transactions above 50$ can obtain a free delivery

How to sign in / sign up? Expand
The steps involved in this process are really simple. All you need to do is:
  • Click on the account icon
  • Click Register to sign up a new account or click Log in if you already have an account

Sellers

Who can sell items? Expand

Only administration account is able to add, edit or delete products from Gtore

Buyers

I want to buy a product - what are the steps? Expand

Buying a product on Gstore is really simple. Each design has a live preview. Once you have selected a graphic design, which is to your liking, you can quickly and securely pay via Paypal. Once the transaction is complete, you gain full access to the purchased product.
